About us

We are bio-informatics students Duncan Huizer, Johanna Veenstra, Pascal Reumer, Sven Staats at Hanze University of Applied Sciences. We strive to learn Bio-informatics and aid the world in its attempt to understand and improve itself.

Why we made this website

This website is made as an assignment to teach ourselves how to implement existing bio-informatics tools and make them more accessible in the form of a website.
The choice for the tool eventually came down to WGD because of its promising use in analysing, dating and visualising DNA duplication events.
For information about WGD visit the background reading page.
