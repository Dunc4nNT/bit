Quick guide on how to use wgd tool.

Step 1:
Upload the .fasta files and press submit.

Step 2:
Select the subtool you want to run and press submit. Here we have chosen dmd which is the default.

Step 3:
Select the files you want to have run and check or fill in any aditional information in the designated boxes.

Step 4:
Now you can select what kind of graphs you would like to have plotted and press submit.

Step 5:
If all went well you now have your graphs. Enjoy your graphs of your .fasta files and happy researching!
