About us

We are bio-informatics students Duncan Huizer, Johanna Veenstra, Pascal Reumer, Sven Staats at Hanze University of Applied Sciences. We strive to learn Bio-informatics and aid the world in its attempt to understand and improve itself.

This website is made as an assignment to teach ourselves how to implement existing bio-informatics tools and make them more accessible in the form of a website. The choice for the tool eventually came down to WGD because of its promising use in analysing and dating DNA sequences. For information about WGD visit the background reading page.

This website has been made possible with courtesy of Hanze University of Applied Sciences and its teachers of class BFV1.
